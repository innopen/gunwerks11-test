Advanced Product Reviews

A highly customizable product advanced reviews system simplifies making a purchase decision for your customers by reading online product reviews.

  • Import/Export review from other review system.
  • Reviews can be like or dislike by the registered users.
  • Verified Buyer badge (Show Verified tag if customer purchased product)
  • Admin have full control on reviews.
